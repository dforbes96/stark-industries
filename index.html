Howard Stark

Founder of the company that brands his name, Howard Stark found Stark Industries in October of 1943 during World War II. The company quickly became an established military contractor after producing 100,000 planes for the Allied Forces as well as creating futuristic military technologies. Under Howard Stark's direction, Stark Industries branched out into numerous scientific fields such as mechatronics, microtechnology, and fringe science.

Anthony "Tony" Stark

Son and successor of Howard Stark, Tony Stark took over as CEO of Stark Industries a few years after the sudden death of his Father. In recent years, Tony Stark has reduced the company's focus on weapons manufacturing and shifted its resources toward providing clean energy.
